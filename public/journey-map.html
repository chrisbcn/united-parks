The Orca Rule: Persistent Constraint
Hard constraints set at onboarding are visible and protected across the full journey. No reroute, disruption response, or GEO offer can remove a protected experience. If the concierge cannot honour the constraint and still improve the plan, it says so and hands the decision back to the guest. The lock indicator persists across all screens.
